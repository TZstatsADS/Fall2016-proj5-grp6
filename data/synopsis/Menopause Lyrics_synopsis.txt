
"Menopause" Synopsis - Broadway musical                                  
    The story setting is conducted exclusively in the format of songs. In the plot, the main characters walk through the store of Bloomingdale and perform 25 songs about love to chocolate, tides of heat, amnesia, copulative difficulties and even more. Most of lyrical works are parodies of popular songs of baby boomers era. In particular, the audience was lucky enough to see and to hear such classic musical numbers like Stayin' Awake, I Heard It Thru and others. Off-Broadway success of this theatrical launched the creation of many histrionics of this piece across the USA. Musical visited many cities in the US, including Eastern ones Chicago & Boston, along with Western: LA, Las Vegas & Denver.
