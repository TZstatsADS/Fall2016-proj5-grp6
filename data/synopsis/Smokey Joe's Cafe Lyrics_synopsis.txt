
"Smokey Joe's Cafe" Synopsis - Broadway musical                                  
    Staging is a revue, consisting of 39 pieces of music. They were created by the legendary creative duo Jerry Leiber & Mike Stoller in 50-60 years of XX century. At the time, the composition were included in the repertoire of such famous singers like Elvis Presley, Tom Jones, Peggy Lee, Bobby Darin, band The Drifters and others. This revue differs with complete absence of storyline and dialogues. The show is a so-called "jukebox" where all the songs follow each other in a single stream. Classic American hits of rhythm and blues and rock'n'roll are performed by five men and four women.
